what is foss cell?

 

Free and open-source software (FOSS) cell is a group of students passionate and enthusiastic about free software and technology. 

The cell frequently conducts coding contests and sessions on open-source software. The cell strives to bring more student developers into the open-source community. They also provide mentorship for student projects. 

what are the objectives ?


1. Awareness on free and open source softwares.

2. Promote the use of free and open source softwares

3  Support free software movement.

4. Conduct training programs and technical events for students.
